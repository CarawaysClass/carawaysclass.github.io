#Better Presentations For Your Students: Take Away  

##Planning Your Presentation  

1. Know your audience (students)  
    - Traditional vs. non-traditional  
    - Accommodations?
    - Preference for analog or digital  
2. Know your room  
    - <a href="http://www.presentationzen.com/presentationzen/2005/02/keep_the_lights.html" target="_blank">Lighting</a>   
		- close the blinds
		- take care when turning lights off  
    - Room layout / dimensions  
    - Pull down screen or wall projection  
    - Instructor station  
3. Tech recommendation:  Presentation Remote   
    - <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Kensington-Wireless-Presenter-Pointer-K33374USA/dp/B000FPGP4U/ref=as_li_ss_il?ie=UTF8&keywords=logitech%20presentation%20remote&qid=1461898647&ref_=sr_1_13&s=electronics&sr=1-13&linkCode=li1&tag=brucecarnet-20&linkId=a008d75be069d87af8ea478ecbbf8176" target="_blank">Kensington Wireless Presenter (K33374USA) $30 at Amazon.com</a>  
    - <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Logitech-910-001354-Wireless-Presenter-R400/dp/B002GHBUTK/ref=as_li_ss_il?ie=UTF8&keywords=logitech%20presentation%20remote&qid=1461898647&ref_=sr_1_1&s=electronics&sr=1-1&linkCode=li2&tag=brucecarnet-20&linkId=fad8a2216a5683a3faff7feb92222c87" target="_blank">Logitech Presenter R400 $35 at Amazon.com</a>  

##Building Your Presentation   

1. <a href="http://www.garrreynolds.com/preso-tips/design/" target="_blank">Minimal word count</a>  
2. When using publisher provided slides  
    - Reposition content  
    - Look for higher resolution image resources  
    - Copyright and Attribution  
    - Removing backgrounds from images
		- Keynote (Mac):  use <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ercm3yJPaeM" target="_blank">Instant Alpha</a>
		- PowerPoint (Win):  use <a href="https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Change-the-color-or-transparency-or-recolor-a-picture-8a068816-81bc-45a1-b633-834f068e6544" target="_blank">Set Transparent Color</a> under the Color menu  
	- Text and Background Images
		- Quick Fix:  add a swatch of semi-opaque color between the  background image and the text  
3. PowerPoint Alternatives  
	- <a href="http://prezi.com" target="_blank">Prezi.com</a>  
	- <a href="http://www.haikudeck.com" target="_blank" >HaikuDeck.com</a>  
	- <a href="http://slides.com" target="_blank">Slides.com</a>
	- <a href="http://www.decksetapp.com/" target="_blank">DeckSet App for Mac</a>

##Delivering Your Presentation  
1. Use the Presenter mode (<a href="https://www.lynda.com/iWork-tutorials/Using-presenter-display/157029/171670-4.html" target="_blank" >Keynote Presenter</a> | <a href="https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Use-Presenter-view-798dcb21-603c-43c3-a782-9a2d7720ab33" target="_blank">PowerPoint</a>) 
2. Use <a href="http://www.netop.com/classroom-management-software/support/training-tutorials.htm" target="_blank">NetOp Vision software</a> to better manage your computer lab classes
3. Zoom your screen 
	- Mac:  <a href="http://www.wikihow.com/Zoom-in-on-a-Mac" target="_blank">Accessibility - Zoom</a>
	- Windows:  <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/enable/aging/magnify.aspx" target="_blank">Magnify tool</a>
