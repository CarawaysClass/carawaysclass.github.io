When does the instructor work?

Generally, I am working Monday through Friday (noon). I will check in some over the weekends, but may or may not grade, answer questions, post News updates, etc. All of my pertinent contact information is posted under Instructor Info.

How long will I have to wait before my assignment grade is posted?

My goal is to grade and return assignments back to you ASAP. In most cases, you should expect a 3–5 day turnaround. If the assignment has a higher complexity or is just “bigger”, it may take me longer to get the grade back to you. If you have not received your grade in a reasonable amount of time, please feel free to contact me. I will be happy to update you on my grading progress.

Are there any due dates for this course?

Check D2L and the SIMnet system for absolute due dates. There are also recommended or suggested due dates that will help you stay on track. Make sure you keep an eye on the News tool, Slack, and your D2L email for reminders, updates, and changes throughout the semester.

Do I need to purchase books for this course? After all, it is online!

Usually, the answer is 'Yes, you will need to purchase your textbook.' This semster I am piloting (i.e., trying out) a new digital textbook from McGraw-Hill and they are providing free access for my students. Pretty cool, huh?!? :)

Do I need to own a computer for this course?

You do not need to own a computer, but you will need to have access to one so that you can install and use the software for this course. No exception will be made for students without reliable access to a computer. After all, this is a computer course.

What do I do if I get really behind?

As long as you stay on task and follow the recommended due dates, you should not fall behind. If, at any time, you feel that you are struggling and need help getting back on track, please contact me.

How do I know what I made in the class?

Visit the Grades page (found in the menubar above). All grades for this course will be posted there. You will have access to your grades all semester. Grading normally takes 3–5 days, with the exception of holidays.

I’m not that familiar with D2L, how do I turn in assignments?

We will use the dropbox feature to submit your assignments. You can find step-by-step instructions under the Resources > D2L module to the left.
