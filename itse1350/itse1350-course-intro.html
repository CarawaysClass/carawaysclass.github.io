Class Introduction

Overview

This course is designed to provide you, the student, with the foundational knowledge required of a beginning systems analyst. It also serves as a guide to the process of information systems development. The course is organized around five phases:

  1. planning
  2. analysis
  3. design
  4. implementation
  5. support and security

    6. As an online course, you will follow a unique learning path of relevant readings, multimedia examples, and opportunities to participate in case simulations. By the end of the course, you will learn how to translate business requirements into information systems that support an organization's objectives.  

    Generally (and per the course catalog), we will cover a...

    Comprehensive introduction to the planning, design, and construction of computer information systems using the systems development life cycle and other appropriate design tools.

    So, let's get to IT!
