ITSE 1311 Beginning Web Programming Semester Project - Part 1

Assignment Overview

This assignment is worth 50% of your Semester Project grade. In this project you will create two design documents of your site: a site map and a wireframe.  You will also begin to build out the rest of your personal web site based on these designs.

Specifications

  1. Create a site map of each of the pages of your site using a tool like Lucidchart or even pen and paper.  This site map should, at a minimum, identify each of the pages of your site and briefly describe what is found on those pages.
  2. Create a wireframe of each of the pages of your site.  Again, you may use a tool like Lucidchart or Mockingbird, or even pen and paper.  
  3. Based on your wireframes, create the remaining pages of your site and upload these to your SiteGround.com hosted site - this will be in addition to the pages you have already deployed.
  4. If you haven't already, add an external stylesheet (.css file) that is referenced by each of your .html pages. This will help in creating a consistent "look and feel" for your site.
  5. Be consistent in the set of navigation buttons (i.e., your menu) that should appear across all of the pages of your site. The placement of these should also be consistent between pages.
  6. Your site should consist of at least 4 pages, including the Home (index.html) and Contact page. You can include your Resume page if that still “fits” within the overall goal your website.

Deliverables

  1. Upload a copy of your site map into this dropbox.  If you used the "pen and paper" approach, take a photo of your map and upload that into the dropbox.
  2. Upload a copy of your wireframe into this dropbox. Similarly, upload a photo of your hand-drawn diagram if that was your approach.
  3. Include the URL to your site as a comment when you upload your file.
