Week 5 - CSS for Behavior

Overview

Read chapter 17 and 18 of our textbook. Be sure to follow along with author's example of transitions and transformations. After reading, complete the Week 5 Quiz. The remainder of the week will be devoted completing the last of our programming challenges (Animating Content) and part 1 of our semester project, designing your web site.

Objectives

At the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Create smooth transitions.
  • Move, rotate, and scale elements.
  • Combine transitions and transforms.
  • Animate content.
  • Apply a CSS reset.
  • Replace text with images
  • Use CSS sprites
  • Use media queries for responsive design

Learning Activities

  1. Read Chapter 17: Transitions, Transforms, and Animation.
  2. Read Chapter 18: CSS Techniques

Assignments

  1. Complete the Week 5 Quiz.
  2. Complete the Programming Challenge: Animating Content.
  3. Begin working on your Semester Project: Part 1.
