ITSE 1311 Beginning Web Programming Semester Project - Part 1

Assignment Overview

This assignment is worth 180 points. In this project you will create two design design documents of your site.  You will also begin to build out the rest of your personal web site based on this design.

Specifications

  1. Create a site map of each of the pages of your site using a tool like Lucidchart or even pen and paper.  This site map should, at a minimum, identify each of the pages of your site and briefly describe what is found on those pages.
  2. Create a wireframe of each of the pages of your site.  Again, you may use a tool like Lucidchart or Mockingbird, or even pen and paper.  
  3. Construct the initially draft pages of your site and upload these to your SiteGround.com hosted site, building on what you have already deployed there.
  4. Though this isn't the final version of your site, be consistent in the set of fonts and colors that you use in the creation of these page.
  5. Be consistent in the set of navigation buttons (i.e., your menu) that should appear across all of the pages of your site. The placement of these should also be consistent between pages.
  6. Your site should consist of at least 4 pages, including the Home (index.html) and Contact page. You can include your Resume page if that still “fits” within the overall goal your website.
  7. Use an external style sheet for the majority of your styling.

Deliverables

  1. Upload a copy of your site map into this dropbox.  If you used the "pen and paper" approach, scan your map and upload that into the dropbox.
  2. Upload a copy of your wireframe into this dropbox.  If you used the "pen and paper" approach, scan your diagram and upload that into the dropbox.
  3. Include the URL to your site as a comment when you upload your file.
