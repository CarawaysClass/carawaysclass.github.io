***DRAFT*** Capstone Project 3: PowerPoint

Description

In this project, you will demonstrate the PowerPoint skills that you have learned in the past several weeks. Up to this point your SIMnet assignments have been very specific as to what you had to do to complete the assignment. In this capstone project, you will be given much more freedom to choose what your submission will look like and what it will include.

Learning Outcomes

At the end of this project, you will be able to:

  • Apply specialized formatting to your PowerPoint presentations
  • Make your PowerPoint presentations easier to understand

Grading Criteria

This capstone project is worth 50 points or 5% of your overall grade. Your submission will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Originality: 10 pts
  • Grammar & Spelling: 5 pts
  • Neatness and Readability: 5 pts
  • Key Features/Functionalities: 20 pts
  • Creativity (Wow Factor): 10 pts
