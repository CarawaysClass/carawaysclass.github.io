Capstone Project 3: PowerPoint

Description

In this project, you will demonstrate the PowerPoint skills that you have learned in the past couple of weeks. Up to this point your SIMnet assignments have been very specific as to what you had to do to complete the assignment. In this capstone project, you will be able to choose what your presentation will look like and what it will include.

Learning Outcomes

At the end of this project, you will be able to:

  • Apply specialized formatting to your PowerPoint presentations
  • Make your PowerPoint presentations easier to understand
  • Understand how to use the presenter mode and related tools
  • Organize your story and thoughts into an effective presentation
  • Prepare and rehearse your presentation script
  • Record a screencast of your presentation

Assignment Tasks

  1. Selecting from a list of suggested topics, you will design, develop, and deliver a personalized PowerPoint presentation. Be sure to create a presentation that is uniquely yours - i.e., be original. Here are the topics that you can choose from:

    • Emerging Technology - Finding inspiration from our Innovation Room (at LSC-UP), select one of the pieces of technology that you find interesting. (For my online students, let me know if you are unable to visit the Innovation Room on campus.)
    • Internet of Things (IoT) Technology - Review the list of the IoT technologies found in the IOTList.co that we used a few weeks ago and select one specific item that you would like to research further.
    • Ethical Issues in Computing - Reearch recent news or current events to find a story of interest about which you could create a presentation.
    • My Field Application - Building off of what you created for our recent Application Software Lab, create a compelling presentation about that software.
    • Specialized Input/Output Device - Share a specific piece of input or output technology that you currently use. This should be something other than your traditional keyboard, mouse, or computer monitor.

  2. Using Microsoft PowerPoint, create an interesting, compelling, and professionaly looking presentation (based on the topic you selected in step 1) that is at least 15 slides in length. The content of your presentation should be original.

  3. In creating your presentation, you should include all of the following:
    1. Appropriate presentation theme (colors, fonts, etc.)(1.4, 2.7, 3.1)
    2. Custom Headers and/or Footers (1.5)
    3. Embedded images (1.6, 2.3)
    4. Effective transition effects (1.7)
    5. Tables to organize information (2.4, 2.5, 2.6)
    6. Animations to add interest (3.2)
    7. SmartArt and/or WordArt effects (2.2, 2.6)
  4. Record a screencast of you presenting your PowerPoint presentation and post this to YouTube. Your video should be at least 3 minutes long.

Grading Criteria

This capstone project is worth 50 points or 5% of your overall grade. Your submission will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Originality: 10 pts
  • Neatness and Readability: 10 pts
  • Compelling and creative message/story: 15 pts
  • Professional and Clearly Presented (as a video): 15 pts

Submitting Your Work

You must submit both of the following, or I will not grade your submission.

  1. Save your final document as a PowerPoint .pptx file with a filename that follows the naming convention: Capstone3-AB.pptx where the last two letters (AB) are your initials. Upload the .pptx file to this Dropbox by the designated Due Date.
  2. Post your video screencast in the #capstone3-video Slack channel.
