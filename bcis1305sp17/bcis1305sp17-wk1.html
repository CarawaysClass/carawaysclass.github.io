Week 1 - SIMnet, Windows 10, MS Office, and Word

Overview

Now that you have started to build some familiarity with our course per our syllabus and making your introductions, we are ready to register and access the SIMnet system. From there we begin by looking at Microsoft Office 2016 and then work through Chapter 1 of Microsoft Word 2016. In this chapter, we look at creating, opening, and saving a Word document. Additionally, we investigate the paragraph and formatting functionality that is essential to a word processing software like Word.

Objectives

At the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Register and Login to SIMnet.
  • Recognize key features of Windows 10.
  • Use the basic features of Office 2016 and navigate the Office 2016 working environment.
  • Create, save, close, and open Office files.
  • Customize the view and display size in Office applications and work with multiple Office files.
  • Print, share, and customize Office files.
  • Use the Ribbon, tabs, groups, dialog boxes, task panes, galleries, and the Quick Access toolbar of any Office application.
  • Use context menus, mini toolbars, keyboard shortcuts, and function keys in Office applications.
  • Organize and customize Windows folders and Office files.
  • Create, save, and open a Word document.
  • Customize a document by entering and selecting text, using word wrap, and using AutoComplete, AutoCorrect, and AutoFormat features.
  • Enhance a document using paragraph breaks, line breaks, spaces, and non-breaking spaces.
  • Edit a document using cut, copy, paste, the Clipboard, and the undo, redo, and repeat features.
  • Customize a document using different fonts, font sizes, and attributes.
  • Enhance a document using text alignment and line and paragraph spacing.
  • Finalize a document using Wordâ€™s research and proofing tools.
  • Apply custom document properties to a document.
