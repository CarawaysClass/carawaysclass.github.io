Emerging Tech Lab

Description

In this lab, you will take the information that you recently researched and learned about on the topic of Emerging Technology - either in the Innovation Room or based on your personal research.

Learning Objectives

At the end of this lab, you will be able to:

  • Identify different types and examples of Emerging Technologies.
  • Describe how certain Emerging Technologies work and how they are used.
  • Describe the effect of Emerging Technologies on our personal lives.
  • Explain how Emerging Technologies are impacting our work.

Assignment Tasks

  1. If you are in my face-to-face class or if the University Park campus is convenient for your to visit, plan to spend at least an hour in the Innovation Room found on the 7th floor of Building familiarizing yourself with the different technologies found there.
  2. For my online students who may not have the option to visit the Unversity Park campus, I've provided several resource links below for you to begin learning about some of the technologies.
  3. Do your own research on those two or three technologies that you find most interesting.
  4. Download and save a copy of the Emerging Technology Lab template document.
  5. Fill out the Emerging Technology Lab document by adding the specific information about two of the Technologies that you selected.

Submitting Your Work

  1. Save your final document as a Word .docx file with a filename that follows the naming convention: EmergingTechLab-AB.docx where the last two letters (AB) are your initials.
  2. Upload the .docx file to this Dropbox by the designated Due Date.
