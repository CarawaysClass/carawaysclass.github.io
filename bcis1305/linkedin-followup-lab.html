Your LinkedIn Profile Should Grow With You

As you develop new skills, acquire knowledge, and build experiences, you should always consider adding these to your LinkedIn profile. It is also a good practice to read through your LI profile regularly (i.e., every month or two) making sure it is complete, up-to-date, and reflects your current professional persona.
