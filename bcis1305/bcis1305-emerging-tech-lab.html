Emerging Tech Lab

Description

In this lab, you will take the information that you recently researched and learned about the topic of Emerging Technology - either in the Innovation Room or based on your personal research.

  8. VIDEOS - DIY Learn Electronics and Arduino Just By Watching These Videos

Learning Objectives

At the end of this lab, you will be able to:

  • Identify different types and examples of Emerging Technologies.
  • Describe how certain Emerging Technologies work and how they are used.
  • Describe the effect of Emerging Technologies on our personal lives.
  • Explain how Emerging Technologies are impacting our work.

Assignment Tasks

  1. If you are in my face-to-face class or if the University Park campus is convenient for you to visit, plan to spend at least an hour in the Innovation Room found on the 7th floor of Building 13 familiarizing yourself with the different technologies found there.
  2. For my online students who may not have the option to visit the University Park campus, provided above.
  3. Do your own research on two or three technologies that you find most interesting.
  4. Download and save a copy of the Emerging Technology Lab template document.
  5. Fill out the Emerging Technology Lab document by adding the specific information about two of the Emerging Technologies that you find most interesting. Be sure to replace the place-holder text identified by the [square brackets].

Submitting Your Work

  1. Save your final document as a Word .docx file with a filename that follows the naming convention: EmergingTechLab-AB.docx where the last two letters (AB) are your initials.
  2. Upload the .docx file to the related SIMnet resource assignment by the designated Due Date.
