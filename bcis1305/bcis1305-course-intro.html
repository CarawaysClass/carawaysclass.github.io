Class Introduction

Overview

This course is designed to equip you, the student, with foundational skills in the use of business productivity software like Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.  We will also look at how web technologies can make you more efficient in finding, acquiring, and using information to help you in your educational and business careers.  Along the way, we'll learn about the ethics of business computing and investigate some of the newest trends in technology.  

Generally (and per the course catalog), this course...

introduces and develops foundational skills in applying essential and emerging business productivity information technology tools. The focus of this course is on business productivity software applications, including word processing, spreadsheets, databases, presentation graphics, data analytics, and business-oriented utilization of the internet.

So, it's time to get your GEEK ON!

Objectives

At the end of this module, you will be able to:
