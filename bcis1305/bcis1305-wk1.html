Week 1 - SIMnet, Slack.com, and Hardware

Overview

Now that you have started to build some familiarity with our course per our syllabus and making your introductions, we are ready to register and access the SIMnet system.

Objectives

At the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Register and Login to SIMnet.
  • Recognize key features of Windows 10.
  • Use the basic features of Office 2016 and navigate the Office 2016 working environment.

Learning Activities

  1. Watch any of the awesome Videos to help prepare you for your chapter reading. I've selected some great videos for you to enjoy. Your textbook is not the only resource at your disposable. Feel free to seek answers from a variety of sources and, if you are compelled, share those via Slack.
  2. Check out the infographic that identifies some key skills that we just might cover in our class. *wink* *wink*
  3. Read ‘Intro to Windows 10, Office 2016, and File Management’ Chapter (SIMBook) in SIMnet.
  4. Complete the end-of-chapter questions to check your knowledge (you will not be required to submit these).
  5. Review any of the Word Cheat Sheets or Docs for personal reference. You can and probably will refer to these throughout the course.

Assignments

  1. Register for access to the SIMnet system.
  2. In SIMnet, complete ‘Intro to Windows 10…’ Chapter SIMBook Let Me Try in-reading tasks.
