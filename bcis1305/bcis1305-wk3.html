Microsoft Word

Overview

In our 3rd week, we begin by looking at Microsoft Office 2016 and then work through Chapters 1 and 2 of Microsoft Word 2016. First, we look at creating, opening, and saving a Word document. Additionally, we investigate the paragraph and formatting functionality that is essential to a word processing software like Word.

Once we have the basics in place, we look at the layout, margins, pagination, and page and section breaks. These powerful features of Word can make your life much easier when working with longer, multi-page documents.

Objectives

Recognize key features of Windows 10.

  • Customize a document by entering and selecting text, using word wrap, and using AutoComplete, AutoCorrect, and AutoFormat features.
  • Enhance a document using paragraph breaks, line breaks, spaces, and non-breaking spaces.
  • Edit a document using cut, copy, paste, the Clipboard, and the undo, redo, and repeat features.
  • Customize a document using different fonts, font sizes, and attributes.
  • Enhance a document using text alignment and line and paragraph spacing.

  • Format a document by customizing margins, page orientation, paper size, and vertical alignment.

  • Enhance document layout by inserting page numbers, headers, and footers.
