Microsoft Word

Overview

This week we begin by looking at Microsoft Office 2016 and then work through Chapters 1 and 2 of Microsoft Word 2016. First, we look at creating, opening, and saving a Word document. Additionally, we investigate the paragraph and formatting functionality that is essential to a word processing software like Word.

Once we have the basics in place, we look at the layout, margins, pagination, and page and section breaks. These powerful features of Word can make your life much easier when working with longer, multi-page documents.

Objectives

At the end of this week, you will be able to:

Recognize key features of Windows 10.
  • Improve overall document design and format with borders, shading, horizontal lines, and hyperlinks.
