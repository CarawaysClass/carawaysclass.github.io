IoT and Privacy

Overview

In last week's research on Emerging Technology, you may have come across the term Internet of Things, or IoT. Or, you may already know about IoT and are actively using it at your home, at work, or both. If this is a new term for you, here's a quick How It Works video that introduces the topic.

With the power of IoT, there are also some concerns about privacy and security. Questions arise around the consequences of the widespread presence of this technology? And does technology make it easy for others to take advantage of our privacy?

Objectives

At the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Describe the capabilities of IoT technologies.
  • Discuss the primary privacy issues IoT.
